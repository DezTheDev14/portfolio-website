My Journey

After graduating from college, I tried my hand at a myriad of different careers across several industries. From energy, to finance, to the legal field. I found myself dissatisfied with several of my previous career choices and was in search of something more meaningful in my life.

Given the fact that technology and the digital space will be increasingly integral parts of society as we move forward into the future, I thought it prudent to think about careers in the same vein. I became enthralled by the prospects of Software Development and the things that I would be able to create. I liked what I was reading, but I didn't quite know where to start. And that's when I stumbled across Thinkful.

With the guiding hand of Thinkful, I've undertaken the perpetual journey that is learning to become a capable Software Developer and I couldn't be more excited about what the future holds.
