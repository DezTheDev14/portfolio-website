My Journey

After graduating from college, I pursued several different career paths in the energy, finance and legal fields. Unfortunately, I found myself dissatisfied with my previous career choices and embarked on a new journey to find my passion.

Given the fact that technology and the digital space are increasingly more integral parts of society as we move forward into the future, I wanted to be a part of making a difference. I thought it was prudent to think about careers which heavily utilized these skill sets. I became enthralled by the prospects of Software Development and the things that I would be able to create. I researched the disciplines and I liked what I read. However, I didn't quite know where to begin and that's when I stumbled across Thinkful.

With the guiding hand of Thinkful, I've undertaken the perpetual journey that encompasses learning to become a capable Software Developer and I couldn't be more excited about what the future holds and my role in making it happen.
